Making makers make

Hi, I am a designer specialized in collaborative prototyping. I make tools to help others be more creative and find solutions to wicked problems, through engagement and empowerment. I like to tinker and experiment, but can also contribute to strategy. I give talks and workshops. I live in Lyon.

  • I founded Modulo, a SAAS tool to help facilitators in their work.
  • I started my own collaborative prototyping studio : POC studio.
  • I'm a tutor at EMLyon Makerslab.
  • I spent 2 years developing the creative and collaborative toolbox at thecamp.
  • I have designed and ran dozens of creative sprints (or hackathons), in museums and in companies, on themes going from artistic video games to banking systems, urban transportation to space exploration.
  • I co-founded Technoculture Club in 2014. An association based in Montreal, Quebec dedicated to helping cultural organizations experiment with technology and their communities.
  • I co-founded the first fablab in central Paris, Le Petit Fablab de Paris, in 2012.
  • As the first employee of nod-A, I constributed to build their value proposition and coined the word Makestorming to describe it.
  • I have been involved in Museomix, the international museum makeathon, since its creation in Paris in 2011.
  • I have been involved in the maker movement as a contributor for the last 10 years. My 3D models and their derivatives have been downloaded 30000+ times.
